About Us

Our Aid

Every year, natural disasters kill around 90,000 people and affect approximately 160 million people worldwide. We recognise that many people want to help out, but are unsure or unwilling to due to the lack of transparency around which many charities operate.

People are unable to keep track of exactly how their money is being used to make a positive impact on communities struck by natural disasters. And here's where we come in..

Here at Our Aid, we believe that natural disaster aid needs to be 3 things:

  • Fast
  • Effective
  • Transparent

By utilising Blockchain technology, donors are now able to keep track of exactly how their money is being spent in aiding those affected by natural disasters. Once their donation is sent, donors can see the flow of money - from their bank account, to suppliers and finally to the aid recipient. At the site of the disaster, donors can view a live-stream of the supplies being distributed and can therefore trust that their donation has been put to good use!
